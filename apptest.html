Wireframe testing

Background Information

You will be testing a wireframe for a potential vocabulary learning app. There are no right or wrong answers. This is not a beauty pageant, and we are not rating things based on looks. Think about whether the actual features of the app meet your needs.

The app was designed for iPhone 6. It may or may not fit your device screen well. That is ok. Pretend it does. Pretend that this is a real app.

Before you begin, please start a screen recording of your phone screen. If you do not know how to do this, click here to learn how to make a screen recording.

Thank you again for participating!

Tasks

  1. You've decided to start practicing Spanish outside of your Spanish 101 course. Open the app, set it up, and learn to use it.
  2. "Hold up," you think. "How can I be successful if I don't even study the basics?" Go study the basics!
  3. You realized that you registered with the name "Test User". However, your name is "Bob User". Correct this error.
  4. There was supposed to be a 4th task, but I forgot. Please inform me of any other tasks you might think would have been good to complete. Or not.
